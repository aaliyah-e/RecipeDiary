Brown Sugar Cookies

Like normal sugar cookies, but just brown.

If you're in the mood for chocolate chip cookies and don't have chocolate chips, or don't like them for some reason, I'd recommend this recipe. These tasted pretty close to that.

Prep Time:

15 minutes

Cook Time:

10 minutes

Total Time:

30 minutes

Servings:

40 cookies

Personal Enjoyment Rating:

★ ★ ★ ★

I knocked off a star because I forgot to add vanilla when it was an ingredient... but I think 4 stars is also an apt score for these too.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 ½ cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups firmly packed dark or light brown sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 (2 sticks) cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt in a medium-size mixing bowl.
  3. Cream the cooled melted butter and sugar together in a large mixing bowl with a wooden spoon until smooth. Add the eggs and vanilla extract and beat until smooth. Stir in the flour mixture until just incorporated. Place the bowl in the fridge for 10 minutes (or up to 6 hours) to let the dough firm up.
  4. Drop the dough by tablespoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheet, leaving about 3 inches between each cookie.
  5. Bake the cookies until golden around the edges but still soft on top, 9 to 11 minutes. Let the cookies stand on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then remove them with a metal spatula to a wire rack to cool completely.
  6. Enjoy!
